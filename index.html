It's not for nothing that Vancouver is considered the rainiest city in Canada, but if you forgot your umbrella or you just don't want to get wet in the rain, visit one of the museums of interest.

  • Learn something new about the world of science with Science World
  • Explore and learn about the sea and the ships with Vancouver Maritime Museum
  • Immerse yourself in the world of art in Vancouver Art Gallery
  • See how Chinatown community helped shape Vancouver`s past, present, and future in Chinatown Storytelling Centre
  • Explorer traditional and contemporary art from First Nations and many other things in Museum of Anthropology
  • Learn the history of Vancouver with Museum of Vancouver
  • Find out how the copper mining took place Britannia Mine Museum
  • Explore the underwater world in Vancouver Aquarium
  • Learn the history of the Inuit with Inuit Gallery of Vancouver
  • Visit former home of the Coroner's Court, City Morgue, and Forensic Analyst LabVancouver Police Museum & Archives

Delicious food, pleasant atmosphere and good music are a great combination for a rainy Vancouver evening. Here is a selection of the best restaurants and pubs in Vancouver with live music.

Photo taken from the official website https://www.guiltandcompany.com/

Guilt&Co

A jazz bar with live music 7 days a week, a cozy atmosphere and a varied menu. You can view the menu and the program of performances on the official website.

Blarney Stone

Is the longest running Irish Pub in the City of Vancouver. In this establishment you will find Irish inspired food Arush entertainment and an Irish atmosphere, and live music is played every Friday, Saturday and Sunday evening.

Photo taken from the official website https://blarneystone.ca/gallery/

Photo taken from the official website https://www.waterstreetcafe.ca/

Water St. Café

This wonderful cafe is located on the second floor of a historic building from 1906. It offers classic Italian cuisine, an excellent wine list, as well as live music in the evenings.

Pourhouse

Pourhouse located in 100-year-old building that was built in 1910 and is furnished in antiques of the era. In this place you will get an indescribable pleasure of great food, drinks and family comfort.

On Thursdays and Sundays there is live music.

Photo taken from the official website https://www.pourhousevancouver.com/

Photo taken from the official website https://www.frankiesjazzclub.ca/

Frankie`s Jazz Club

Showcasing up and coming musicians and anchored by renowned talent from across the continent, Frankie`s Jazz Club is currently presenting live music four nights per week, Thursday through Sunday.

Doors opens at 6:30pm and performances starts at 8pm.

The Lobby Lounge

The Lobby Lounge and Raw Bar is the place where glamour meets comfort. Enjoying the live music by the fireside, from front row seats or mingling at the communal tables.

Photo taken from the official website https://www.lobbyloungerawbar.com/

Photo taken from the official website https://www.wedgewoodhotel.com/

Bacchus Restaurant & Lounge

This is a restaurant at the hotel with a chic expensive atmosphere. In the evenings there is live piano music. You can enjoy it sitting with a cocktail by the fireplace.

The Wolf & Hound

This popular Irish pub, founded in honor of the Irish musician Danny Burns, is famous for delicious food. It offers Irish music on Sundays, jazz pop and rock on Fridays and Saturdays.

The pub's Facebook page says that The Wolf and Hound is Kitsilano's favorite pub next door, serving local cuisine and premium imported delicacies.

Photo taken from the official website https://www.wolfandhound.ca/

The Story Cafe

A pleasant cafe, furnished with vintage things and every thing has its own story. The menu is varied and delicious.

During the week there are musical evenings dedicated to the demonstration of various musical genres and various talented musicians.
